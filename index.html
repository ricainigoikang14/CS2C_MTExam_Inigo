Tourist Spots & Adventures

Vigan City

Vigan is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its preserved Spanish colonial architecture. Walk along Calle Crisologo and feel the history come alive. Enjoy the vibrant local culture and cuisine.

#Vigan #Heritage #CalleCrisologo #Culture #History

Bantay Bell Tower

The Bantay Bell Tower offers panoramic views of Ilocos Sur. Climb the historic tower and witness breathtaking sunsets. A must-visit for photographers and history lovers.

#Bantay #BellTower #Sunset #Photography #History

San Esteban

The San Esteban Church offers panoramic views of Ilocos Sur. Climb the historic tower and witness breathtaking sunsets. A must-visit for photographers and history lovers.

#SanEsteban #Church #Sunset #Photography #History

Baluarte Zoo

Baluarte Zoo is a privately owned zoo in Vigan that showcases various animals, including exotic species. It's a great place for families and animal lovers to learn and have fun.

#Baluarte #Zoo #Vigan #FamilyFriendly #Wildlife

Crisologo Museum

The Crisologo Museum showcases the rich history and culture of Vigan. Explore the ancestral home of the Crisologo family and discover artifacts from the Spanish colonial period.

#Crisologo #Museum #Vigan #History #Culture

St.Paul Metropolitan Cathedral

The St. Paul Metropolitan Cathedral is a stunning example of neo-gothic architecture in Vigan. Its intricate design and beautiful stained glass windows make it a must-visit for art and history enthusiasts.

#StPaul #Cathedral #Vigan #Architecture #History

Pinsal Falls

Pinsal Falls is a beautiful natural attraction in Ilocos Sur, perfect for a refreshing dip and a picnic with family and friends. The scenic views and tranquil atmosphere make it a must-visit.

#PinsalFalls #Nature #Waterfall #IlocosSur #Adventure

Sabangan Cove

Sabangan Cove is a serene beach destination in Ilocos Sur, perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and enjoying the natural beauty of the coastline. Its calm waters make it ideal for families.

#SabanganCove #Beach #IlocosSur #FamilyFriendly #Nature

Abot Cave

Abot Cave is a fascinating natural wonder in Ilocos Sur, featuring stunning rock formations and a serene atmosphere. It's an ideal spot for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

#AbotCave #Nature #Adventure #IlocosSur #Exploration
